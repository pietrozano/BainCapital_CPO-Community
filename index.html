Bain Capital · Procurement AI Maturity Assessment 2026

This tool maps your procurement AI maturity across 9 process steps and 4 dimensions, producing a Composite Maturity Index (CMI). Once you submit, you will see your score benchmarked against the portfolio average and gain full access to all AI use cases identified across Bain Capital portfolio companies.

Step 00 · General
General Procurement Context
Open-ended questions to capture the overall procurement landscape. These provide qualitative context for the assessment and are shared with your BCG team.
Strategic Context & Priorities
1. Is the overall level of process automation in procurement high — or are most activities still manual and paper-based?
2. Does the organization have a defined AI strategy or roadmap for procurement — or are AI initiatives ad hoc and isolated?
3. Is there organizational buy-in and executive sponsorship for AI adoption within the procurement function?
4. What are the biggest procurement pain points the CPO wants to address — and where does AI represent the highest strategic opportunity?
5. To what extent are core procurement skills and capabilities present — analytics, market knowledge, sourcing, SRM — and where do gaps exist?
6. Is procurement data (spend, contract, savings, compliance, supplier) centralized and reliable — or retrieved entity by entity?
